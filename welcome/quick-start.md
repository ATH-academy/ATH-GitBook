# Quick Start Guide

Get started with All Time High Academy in just a few minutes.

---

## Step 1: Create Your Account

You have three options to sign up:

### Email & Password
1. Go to [alltimehigh.academy](https://alltimehigh.academy)
2. Click "Sign Up"
3. Enter your email and create a password
4. Verify your email address

### Discord OAuth
1. Click "Continue with Discord"
2. Authorize All Time High Academy
3. Your account is created automatically

### Whop OAuth
1. Click "Continue with Whop"
2. Sign in to your Whop account
3. Authorize the connection

---

## Step 2: Choose Your Membership

After signing up, you'll be prompted to select a plan:

| Plan | Access Level | Price |
|------|-------------|-------|
| Free | Intro videos only | $0 |
| Community | Full courses + Discord | $67/month |
| VIP | 1-1 Sessions + Everything | $3,500 |

Not sure which plan? Start with **Free** to explore, then upgrade when ready.

---

## Step 3: Complete Your Profile

1. Go to **Settings** in your dashboard
2. Add your profile picture
3. Set your preferred language (English or Spanish)
4. Save your changes

---

## Step 4: Start Your First Course

1. Navigate to **Courses** from the main menu
2. Browse available courses
3. Click on a course to see its modules
4. Start with the first module

### Pro Tips:
- Modules unlock sequentially - complete one to unlock the next
- Your progress is saved automatically
- You can resume where you left off anytime

---

## Step 5: Connect to Discord

For Community, VIP, and Concierge members:

1. Go to your **Dashboard**
2. Find the Discord connection section
3. Click "Connect Discord"
4. Authorize the connection
5. You'll receive an invite to our private community

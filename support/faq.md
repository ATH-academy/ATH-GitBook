# Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about All Time High Academy.

---

## Account & Access

### How do I create an account?
Visit [alltimehigh.academy](https://alltimehigh.academy), click "Sign Up," and choose email, Discord, or Whop to create your account.

### I forgot my password. How do I reset it?
Click "Forgot Password" on the login page, enter your email, and follow the reset link sent to your inbox.

### Can I change my email address?
Currently, email changes require contacting support. We'll verify your identity and update your email.

### How do I connect my Discord account?
Go to Dashboard > Settings > Connections and click "Connect Discord." Authorize the connection in the popup.

### Can I use multiple devices?
Yes! Your account works across all devices. Progress syncs automatically.

## Membership & Billing

### What's the difference between plans?
- **Free**: Intro videos only
- **Community** ($67/mo): Full courses + Discord
- **VIP** ($3,500): 4 private sessions + everything
- **Concierge** ($12,000/yr): 12 sessions + premium support

See [Plan Comparison](../membership/plan-comparison.md) for full details.

### How do I upgrade my plan?
Go to Dashboard > Settings > Membership and click "Upgrade." Select your new plan and complete payment.

### Can I cancel my subscription?
Community members can cancel anytime in Settings > Membership. Access continues until the end of your billing period.

### Do you offer refunds?
- **Community**: No refunds for partial months
- **VIP/Concierge**: Contact us within 14 days to discuss

### What payment methods do you accept?
- Credit/debit cards (Stripe)
- Crypto (USDT) for VIP/Concierge
- Bank transfer for VIP/Concierge

## Courses & Learning

### How do I access courses?
Navigate to "Courses" in the menu. Click any course to see modules and start learning.

### Why is a module locked?
Modules unlock sequentially. Complete the previous module (including passing any quizzes) to unlock the next.

### Can I download videos for offline viewing?
Not yet. Offline viewing is planned for a future update.

### How long do I have access to courses?
Access depends on your membership:
- **Community**: While subscribed
- **VIP**: Lifetime community access
- **Concierge**: During your annual subscription + lifetime community

### Are courses available in Spanish?
Yes! The platform and courses are available in English and Spanish. Switch languages in Settings or the header.

## Quizzes & Certifications

### How do quizzes work?
Quizzes test your knowledge after modules. You must pass to proceed. Multiple-choice questions with immediate feedback.

### What score do I need to pass?
- Short quizzes (3 questions): 67% (2/3)
- Standard quizzes (10 questions): 80% (8/10)

### Can I retake a quiz?
Yes, most quizzes allow unlimited retakes. Just review the material and try again.

### How do I get certified?
Complete all required courses, pass all quizzes, and meet score thresholds. Submit for review when eligible.

### What certifications are available?
- **ATH-CCP**: Certified Crypto Professional
- **ATH-CDS**: Certified DeFi Specialist
- **ATH-CMA**: Certified Market Analyst

### Do certifications expire?
Currently no. We may introduce renewal requirements in the future.

## Technical Issues

### Videos won't play. What do I do?
1. Check your internet connection
2. Try a different browser (Chrome, Firefox, Brave)
3. Clear browser cache
4. Disable VPN if active
5. Contact support if issue persists

### My progress isn't saving.
1. Stay connected during learning
2. Don't close browser mid-video
3. Refresh the page
4. Log out and back in
5. Report to support with details

### The site is loading slowly.
1. Check your internet speed
2. Close other browser tabs
3. Clear cache and cookies
4. Try a different browser
5. Disable browser extensions

### I can't access content I should have.
1. Verify your subscription is active
2. Check your access level
3. Ensure previous modules are complete
4. Log out and back in
5. Contact support

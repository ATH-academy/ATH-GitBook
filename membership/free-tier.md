## Getting Started

### Step 1: Create Account
1. Visit [alltimehigh.academy](https://alltimehigh.academy)
2. Click "Sign Up"
3. Choose email, Discord, or Whop
4. Verify your email

### Step 2: Explore
1. Browse the course catalog
2. Watch available intro videos
3. Review course outlines
4. Check out the dashboard

### Step 3: Evaluate
- Is this the right platform for you?
- Do the courses match your goals?
- Is the teaching style effective?

## Upgrading from Free

### To Community ($67/month)
1. Go to Dashboard > Settings
2. Click "Upgrade to Community"
3. Enter payment details
4. Access unlocked immediately

### To VIP ($3,500)
1. Go to Dashboard > Settings
2. Click "Upgrade to VIP"
3. Complete payment (or book a call)

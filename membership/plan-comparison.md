## Choosing Your Path

### Decision Framework

**Choose Free if:**
- You're just exploring crypto education
- You want to test the platform first
- Budget is a primary concern

**Choose Community if:**
- You're a self-directed learner
- You want structured curriculum
- Community support is valuable to you
- Monthly commitment works better

**Choose VIP if:**
- You have capital ready to invest
- You want personalized guidance
- You learn better with 1-1 attention
- You're serious about crypto success

**Choose Couples if:**
- You and a partner want to learn together
- You're making joint investment decisions
- You want aligned strategies

**Choose Concierge if:**
- You have significant capital ($100k+)
- You want ongoing, premium support
- You prefer in-person guidance
- You value white-glove service

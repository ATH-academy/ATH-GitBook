# Creating Your Account

Set up your All Time High Academy account in minutes.

---

## Sign Up Options

### Option 1: Email & Password

The traditional way to create an account:

1. Visit [alltimehigh.academy](https://alltimehigh.academy)
2. Click **"Sign Up"** or **"Get Started"**
3. Enter your email address
4. Create a strong password (minimum 8 characters)
5. Click **"Create Account"**
6. Check your email for a verification link
7. Click the link to verify your account

**Password Requirements:**
- Minimum 8 characters
- Recommended: Mix of letters, numbers, and symbols

---

### Option 2: Discord OAuth

Sign up instantly with your Discord account:

1. Click **"Continue with Discord"**
2. If not logged into Discord, enter your credentials
3. Review the permissions requested
4. Click **"Authorize"**
5. Your account is created automatically

**Benefits:**
- One-click signup
- Automatic Discord community connection
- No password to remember

---

### Option 3: Whop OAuth

For users with Whop accounts:

1. Click **"Continue with Whop"**
2. Log into your Whop account if needed
3. Authorize All Time High Academy
4. Account created with your Whop profile

**Benefits:**
- Seamless subscription management
- Unified billing through Whop
- Easy membership upgrades

---

## After Signing Up

### Complete Your Profile

1. Go to **Dashboard > Settings**
2. Add your profile information:
   - Display name
   - Profile picture (optional)
   - Bio (optional)
3. Set your preferences:
   - Preferred language (English/Spanish)
   - Notification settings
4. Click **Save**

### Choose Your Plan

After account creation, you'll see the plan selection:

| Plan | Price | What You Get |
|------|-------|--------------|
| Free | $0 | Intro videos, limited content |
| Community | $67/month | Full courses + Discord |
| VIP | $3,500 | 4x 1-1 sessions + everything |

Select the plan that fits your needs. You can always upgrade later.

## Account Security

### Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)

We strongly recommend enabling 2FA:

1. Go to **Settings > Security**
2. Enable two-factor authentication
3. Scan the QR code with Google Authenticator
4. Enter the verification code
5. Save your backup codes securely

### Password Reset

If you forget your password:

1. Click **"Forgot Password"** on the login page
2. Enter your email address
3. Check your inbox for the reset link
4. Click the link and create a new password

---

## Common Issues

### Email Not Verified

If you can't log in:
1. Check your spam/junk folder
2. Click "Resend Verification Email" on the login page
3. Wait a few minutes and check again

### OAuth Connection Failed

If Discord or Whop login fails:
1. Make sure you're logged into the service
2. Clear your browser cache
3. Try again in a new browser window
4. Contact support if the issue persists

### Account Already Exists

If you see "account already exists":
- You may have signed up before
- Try logging in instead
- Use "Forgot Password" if needed

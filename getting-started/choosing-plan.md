## How to Choose

### Ask Yourself:

1. **What's your learning style?**
   - Self-paced → Community
   - Guided → VIP or Concierge

2. **What's your investment capital?**
   - < $10,000 → Community
   - $10,000 - $100,000 → VIP
   - > $100,000 → Concierge

3. **Do you need personalized guidance?**
   - No → Community
   - Yes, some → VIP
   - Yes, ongoing → Concierge

4. **What's your timeline?**
   - Exploring → Free
   - Ready to learn → Community
   - Ready to deploy capital → VIP

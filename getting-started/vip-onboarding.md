## Accounts & Platforms

### 7. Twitter/X Account

Create a Twitter account for crypto news and alpha:

**Steps:**
1. Download the Twitter/X app on your phone
2. Create an account (or use existing)
3. Follow crypto accounts we'll recommend in session

> Twitter is the primary source for real-time crypto information and alpha.

### 8. Binance Exchange Account

Register on Binance for trading:

**Registration:** [binance.com](https://www.binance.com/activity/referral-entry/CPA?ref=CPA_00WHJAE3QA)

**Required Verification:**
- Government ID (cedula)
- Proof of residence (utility bill)

**Note:** Complete KYC verification to unlock full features and higher limits.

### 9. Google Authenticator (2FA)

Set up two-factor authentication:

**Download:**
- iOS: [App Store](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/google-authenticator/id388497605)
- Android: [Google Play](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.google.android.apps.authenticator2)

**Enable on Binance:**
1. Go to Binance > Account > Security
2. Select "Authenticator App"
3. Scan QR code with Google Authenticator
4. Save backup codes securely

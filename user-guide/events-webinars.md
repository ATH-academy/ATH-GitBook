# Webinars & in-person events

The platform includes **public landing pages** for webinars and **in-person events**, with registration and dedicated links.

---

## Routes in the app

| Type | Listing / entry | Invite page |
|------|-----------------|-------------|
| Webinar | `/webinar` | `/webinar/:slug` |
| In-person event | `/evento` | `/evento/:slug` |

Each **slug** identifies a specific event (for example a campaign or date). The exact content (title, date, CTA) is configured by the team in the admin dashboard.

---

## What to expect as a visitor

- Event information and a countdown when applicable.
- A registration form or flow depending on how the event is set up.
- A responsive layout on mobile and desktop.

<div class="docsify-callout docsify-callout--info">
<p><strong>Note:</strong> Details for each event (pricing, requirements, language) may vary. Always use the official link shared by the All Time High Academy team.</p>
</div>

---

## Your account

Depending on the event, registration may ask for email only or tie into your membership. If you already have an academy account, sign in when the flow asks for it to avoid duplicate records.

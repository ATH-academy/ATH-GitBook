## Certification Process

### Step 1: Complete Required Courses
1. Enroll in certification track
2. Complete all modules in order
3. Watch all video content
4. Study supplementary materials

### Step 2: Pass Required Quizzes
1. Take assessments after each module
2. Score above minimum threshold
3. Retake if needed to pass
4. All quizzes must be passed

### Step 3: Meet Score Requirements
1. Calculate your average score
2. Ensure you meet minimum (80-85%)
3. Retake low-scoring quizzes if needed

### Step 4: Submit for Review
1. Dashboard shows "Ready for Review"
2. Click "Submit for Certification"
3. Admin reviews your completion
4. Approval typically within 48 hours

### Step 5: Receive Certification
1. Email notification on approval
2. PDF certificate generated
3. Download from your dashboard
4. Add to LinkedIn (coming soon)

## FAQ

### General

**Q: How long does certification take?**
A: Depends on your pace. Most complete ATH-CCP in 4-8 weeks with dedicated study.

**Q: Do certifications expire?**
A: Currently no. Future updates may introduce renewal requirements.

**Q: Can I get certified without 1-1 sessions?**
A: Yes, Community members can earn all certifications through self-study.

### Requirements

**Q: What if I fail a quiz?**
A: Retake it until you pass. Only passing scores count.

**Q: Do I need to complete courses in order?**
A: Yes, modules unlock sequentially. Complete each before proceeding.

**Q: Can I skip to certification exam?**
A: No, all course content must be completed first.

### After Certification

**Q: How do I download my certificate?**
A: Go to Dashboard > Certifications > Download PDF.

**Q: Can I add it to my resume?**
A: Yes! List it as:
  - "ATH-CCP, All Time High Academy, [Year]"

**Q: Is the certification recognized?**
A: It's an industry credential from a professional academy. While not government-issued, it demonstrates verified competence.

## Tips for Success

### Study Strategies
1. **Set a schedule**: Consistent daily/weekly study time
2. **Take notes**: Handwritten notes improve retention
3. **Review before quizzes**: Don't rely on memory alone
4. **Apply concepts**: Practice in real scenarios
5. **Join community**: Discuss with fellow students

### Quiz Preparation
1. Complete all lessons before attempting
2. Review your notes
3. Understand concepts, don't just memorize
4. Take practice attempts if available
5. Sleep well before major assessments

### Staying Motivated
1. Set milestone goals
2. Track progress visually
3. Celebrate completions
4. Connect with community
5. Remember your "why"
